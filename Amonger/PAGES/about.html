Do you like the feeling of being around people in a coffee shop while you're immersed in your own laptop world? What if we told you that we have improved that experience?

Amonger is a "semi-social" mobile app that allows people to be around like-minded individuals without having to interact with them.

With Amonger, you create a network of friends with whom you can essentially curate the people around you as you go about your day.
